THE ARCHITECTURE (Investor Book v1.1)

A QuadRoof system is a residential- or commercial-scale rooftop that combines solar generation + battery storage + (optional) small on-site compute module. The system is owned by an AssetCo SPV. The roof owner grants an easement and buys power under a long-dated PPA. Up to four contracted cash flows are produced.

Homeowner PPA
Utility Capacity
Compute Off-Take (Premium)
Tax-Equity Monetization
Compute is the conditional 4th stream: surplus generation powers on-prem inference for privacy-sensitive / regulated buyers who pay a premium for data that never leaves the controlled environment.
