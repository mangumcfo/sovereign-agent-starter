## Root cause (the metalayer lesson — owned)
1. **The gates encode existence, not calibrated quality.** My gate-completeness work + #4 named "completeness ≠ quality" — but I left quality as a soft "batch metric," not a *blocking, S2-calibrated threshold*. A gate that checks "figures exist" passes 5 figures and 38 figures identically. **That is my miss: I hardened the existence gates and left the quality bar advisory.**
2. **Exemplar-first amplified a light exemplar.** Ch1 was ratified as the bar — but the bar itself was lighter than S2. The rail then faithfully reproduced the light bar across all 8 chapters. **Exemplar-first is dangerous unless the exemplar is calibrated against the anchor (S2) *before* it's ratified.**
3. **"Fewer human touchpoints" optimized away the depth signal.** The flow-model direction reduced human cards before the *quality* enforcement that would make that safe existed.

## The calibrated S2 quality bar (the anchor — encode this, don't re-invent it)
A volume is **S2-comparable** when it meets, at minimum, the S2 *floor* (not the ceiling):
- **Substance:** in the S2 depth band — order **~12k+ words**, worked examples + industry signals per chapter, appendices where S2 carried them. (Not a rigid count — a regression *alarm* when materially below the band.)
- **Figures:** **≥ ~12–15** Visual Standard v2.0 figures (figure-per-major-concept density), not a token few.
- **/seeit:** **core chapters covered** (~8–11 pages), at the Education-Board accessible+grounded depth.
- **Cover:** front/spine/back resolved to the v2.0 standard.
- **PDF/KDP:** production-ready, full format set.

## Rail recalibration (the gate changes — so it can't recur)
1. **Quality-calibrated artifact-package gate.** The `human_review_ready` manifest must check *thresholds vs the S2 anchor*, not mere existence: word-band, figure-count ≥ floor, /seeit coverage ≥ floor, cover-resolved, PDF set. Below the band → gate stays RED + mints an obligation naming the shortfall. (Extends the gate Tiger just added from existence → calibrated.)
2. **Substantive-quality metric becomes BLOCKING, not advisory.** The Cold-Reader/adversarial verdict + the S2-comparison must be a hard gate on `human_review_ready`, not a soft batch-card signal.
3. **Exemplar-calibration discipline.** Before an exemplar (Ch1 / first volume) is ratified as the bar, it is explicitly diffed against the S2 anchor; an exemplar below the anchor cannot be ratified. This stops a light bar from propagating.
4. **The S2 deliverables are the standing quality anchor** — referenced by the gate, re-checked each volume. "Calibrate against actual S2," made mechanical.
