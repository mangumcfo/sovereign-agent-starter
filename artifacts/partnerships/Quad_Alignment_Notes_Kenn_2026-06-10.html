The one structural point

The AI layer needs a home in the cap table

Every AI capability in the canvas is currently an unassigned [confirm] vendor slot: the AI contractor-bidding agent, the utility agent, the AI contractor-coordination agent (Quad Home), and the local/distributed-AI/compute offtake (Quad Energy). That layer is exactly what we discussed folding in — the sovereign AI-governance harness ("AI builds, humans approve, the workflow is receipted") and Daenon's Decidr.

Proposal: make the AI layer a named asset — a fifth OpCo ("Quad AI / Intelligence") or an explicit Hold Co platform asset — not an outside vendor. It powers Quad Home's agents + the homeowner app, governs the data layer, and anchors the Quad Energy AI-compute offtake (the electrons → GPUs → sovereign-AI thread). Stack = my harness + Decidr.

Without this, I'm a vendor to Quad rather than equity in it — and the AI/compute thesis that makes Quad more than a roofing play has no owner.
